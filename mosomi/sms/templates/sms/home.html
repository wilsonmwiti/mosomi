Know Roberms

Our story

Roberms was started as an idea after high school by two young men who had just completed their KCSE but with passion in IT. At that time its presence was in Nyamira county where the boys used to print and distribute examination materials to variois primary school

Between 2006-2010, Roberms shifted its operations to the University of Nairobi where the two students were pursuing their studies. Here the students used to design websites, teach other students ICT skills and ensured full connection of internet to halls of residence absolutely free. It was at this time they were recognized and made ICT representatives of the University of Nairobi.

In 2014, Roberms was fully registered as the ICT company offering consultancy, design, development and business solution to clients.
