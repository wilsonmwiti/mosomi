Welcome to Roberms

Helping businesses during covid-19

Overview

Covid 19 has made many businesses and shops closed or operating at a bare minimal. We will talk about how Roberms can help such businesses serve their customers during such periods . They include restaurants, churches, schools and many more

Restaurants

Roberms has come up with ways of ensuring restaurants and clubs identify, and get a way to reach their most frequent customers. This is through their mpesa till numbers where the club can get the names and phone numbers of their clients. Periodically engage them through messages to do online delivery and inform them on offers during Covid period.

Churches

Churches needs to communicate to their members regularly. You can collect the phone numbers of members and store them in our platform. Then when you want to broadcast anything, share the message. For instance, many churches are going live through youtube and facebook to present their sermons and anything they have. They can send links just before doing that to ensure members catch up with the events.
