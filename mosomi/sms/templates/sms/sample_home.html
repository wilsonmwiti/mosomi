Know Roberms

Our story

Roberms was started as an idea after high school by two young men who had just completed their KCSE. At that time its presence was in Nyamira county where the founders used to print and distribute examination materials to variois primary schools and train KCSE graduates on basic computer skills

Between 2006-2010, Roberms shifted its operations to the University of Nairobi where the two founders were pursuing their studies. Here the students used to design websites, teach other students ICT skills and ensured full connection of internet to halls of residence absolutely free. It was at this time they were recognized and made ICT representatives of the University of Nairobi.

In 2014, Roberms was fully registered as the ICT company offering consultancy, design, development and business solution to clients.

As from 2016, Roberms started offering bulk sms servives, api intergarions, sacco systems, school systems and consultancy.

2019 came as a gift for Rooberms as it integrated all its services to be offered under one system to enable users access all products within one simple application.
